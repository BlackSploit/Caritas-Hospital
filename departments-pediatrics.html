Pediatrics

Pediatric Surgery Caritas St. George Hospital offers comprehensive care for children with surgical needs, ranging from newborns to adolescents. Our services cover a wide array of conditions, including congenital malformations (birth defects) such as cleft lips and various neonatal surgical issues. We boast a team of pediatric specialists available 24/7, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure designed to diagnose, treat, and provide all-encompassing care for our young patients.

Highlights
  • Monitoring of growth and development
  • Eczema, Rashes and skin disorders
  • Allergies and Asthma
  • Blood disorders
  • Intensive Care Unit
  • Correction of Congenital Anomalies
