The Success Story

Aware of the challenges presented by inadequate health care delivery in a country with a vast population, Caritas St. George Hospital was founded with the ambition to establish a health care institution capable of serving the populace and saving lives. Starting with a modest setup including a general practitioner, a gynecologist, and an anesthesiologist on call, it marked a significant stride towards enhancing health care in the region, signifying the onset of a revamped healthcare system and the introduction of a premier medical facility in Idukki, Kerala. The institution continued to broaden its services and incorporate essential divisions, reflecting a steadfast commitment to improving healthcare accessibility and quality.

Over time, the hospital became a distinguished entity among the community and a landmark in elevating the standard of living through superior health care, thus fulfilling its noble objective of offering better health services at fair and accessible prices. This dedication led to the continuous enhancement of the health facility, with expansions that included primary and advanced specialty departments such as cardiology, urology, neurology, facio-maxillo surgery, and reconstructive surgery, catering to a wide range of medical needs.

Transforming from a modest establishment to one of the most comprehensive health care establishments in Kerala, Caritas St. George Hospital. now boasts an extensive team of medical and paramedical personnel, including full-time physicians and visiting physicians. This growth is a testament to its original vision that propels continuous evolution into sophisticated units and robust systems equipped with state-of-the-art technology to enhance health care, featuring advanced diagnostic and treatment options among other notable specialties. Additionally, a new facility, housing all the super-specialty departments, is also nearing completion within the same premises, poised to further extend the hospital's capacity to serve.

From its inception to today, the journey of Caritas St. George Hospital has significantly impacted the healthcare landscape in Kerala. Here, patients are treated with a comprehensive approach to wellness, embodying the true spirit of the institution and its commitment to holistic health care.
