Urology

Urology The Urology Department at Caritas St. George Hospital offers a full range of urological services for diagnosing and treating various urological issues across all age groups, from pediatric to geriatric patients, and for both males and females. It provides both medical and surgical solutions, including emergency urological care.

Highlights
  • Urology Consultation
  • Ureteroscopy (URS)
  • Kidney Stone Treatment
  • Urinary Tract / Bladder Stones Treatment
  • Transurethral Resection of The Prostate (TURP).
  • Transurethral Incision of the Prostate (TUIP).
